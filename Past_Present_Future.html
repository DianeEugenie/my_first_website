Diane's Place

Past, Present and Future Passions and Interests

Past

Born and raised in the Netherlands, where I lived for over 20 years. I decided to move to the United Kingdom after finishing my degree in Linguistics from Leiden University. Not ready to face adulthood just yet, I decided to become an au-pair in London.

Within a couple of weeks there, I become a host/co-organizer for a social event. The event was created for language learners to come together and explore London and its surrounding areas.

After my au-pair days, I worked for a printing company with its international office in Ealing, London. In June 2016, the company moved this office back to Germany. Shortly after, I found a job as a customer service advisor for the LEGO Group where I stayed for over a year and a half.

Present

Currenty, enrolled at CodeClan to start a new career path. My aim is to return to my language learning and linguistic roots. This time I will be delving into the world of artificial languages. It is not completely unknown to me, as I have dipped my toes into the ocean that is programming languages before as well as fictional languages like Na'vi (Avatar) and Klingon (Star Trek).

I would like to find a career in the field where linguistics and computer languages meet. My interests in the field of computing are Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Computational Linguistics.

Future

In the future, I hope to be a programming wiz working for a company that is trying to better the world, especially environmentally so. Hopefully, with the opportunity to travel to different countries and/or work remotely. On the side, I will have created a couple of educational games that help people, especially children, understand the world we live in. Whichever country I end up in is not really of any importance. I will be willing to move wherever is needed, provided that my partner is okay with it too.
